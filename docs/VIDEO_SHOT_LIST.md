## Editing guidance

- Compress generation and replanning waits; never imply that the stages completed faster than the recorded workflow actually did.
- If a live API call is slow, retain the submitted state, cut to the cached successful result, and use the fallback narration in `DEMO_SCRIPT.md`.
- Use straight cuts or short cross-dissolves. Avoid decorative transitions that compete with the interface.
- Keep cursor movement intentional and remove dead time between clicks.
- Do not claim guaranteed employment, guaranteed completion dates, autonomous agents, cloud-synced progress, or public roadmap sharing.
- Keep the final narration at or below 2:55 to allow a small playback or transition margin under three minutes.
